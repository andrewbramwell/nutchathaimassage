About Nutcha Thai Massage Sutton Coldfield

Welcome to Nutcha Thai Massage Sutton Coldfield, which opened in 2019 on Chester Road North. We provide authentic Thai massage therapy to the local community, maintaining the same high standards of care and professional service across all our treatments.

Our Sutton Coldfield location features four treatment rooms, including a couples massage room, with six therapists - four full-time and two part-time practitioners. All our therapists hold VTCT Level 3 Thai Massage certification and have trained with recognised instructors. We offer convenient parking with free 2-hour street parking, or free 3-hour parking at nearby Tesco, Lidl, and Aldi, making it easy to visit us for your massage treatment.
