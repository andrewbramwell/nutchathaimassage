About Nutcha Thai Massage Hinckley

Welcome to Nutcha Thai Massage Hinckley, which opened in 2017 on Coventry Road. We provide authentic Thai massage therapy to the local community, maintaining the same high standards of care and professional service across all our treatments.

Our Hinckley location features three treatment rooms plus a separate couples massage room that can be expanded with extra beds when needed. We have five therapists - two full-time and three part-time practitioners. All our therapists hold VTCT Level 3 Thai Massage certification and have trained with recognised instructors. We offer convenient free parking in our adjacent car park, making it easy to visit us for your massage treatment.
