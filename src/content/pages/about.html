About Nutcha Thai Massage

Our Story

A Passion for Traditional Thai Massage

Nutcha Thai Massage was founded by Nutcha, who brought authentic Thai healing traditions from her home province of Sisaket in northeastern Thailand to the UK. After moving to Britain in 2007, she discovered her passion for traditional Thai massage therapy through formal training in Thailand.

What began as a single treatment room in Stratford-upon-Avon in 2014 has grown into a respected chain of massage locations across the Midlands and Scotland. From our first location, where we started as "Thai Relaxing Massage," we have expanded to six locations, each maintaining the same commitment to authentic Thai healing traditions and professional service.

Today, our focus remains on maintaining consistent quality across all locations while supporting our team's professional development. We are dedicated to providing employment opportunities for skilled Thai massage therapists whilst bringing the therapeutic benefits of traditional Thai massage to communities across Britain.

We treat every client like family, delivering authentic Thai massage with genuine care and professional expertise. Nutcha Owner
