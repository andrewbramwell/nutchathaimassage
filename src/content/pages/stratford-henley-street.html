About Nutcha Thai Massage Stratford Henley Street

Welcome to Nutcha Thai Massage Stratford Henley Street, our original location which opened in 2014 on historic Henley Street. We provide authentic Thai massage therapy to the local community, maintaining the same high standards of care and professional service across all our treatments.

Our Stratford Henley Street location features five treatment rooms with six massage beds, including one couples massage room. We have six therapists - three full-time and three part-time practitioners. All our therapists hold VTCT Level 3 Thai Massage certification and have trained with recognised instructors. Walk-ins are welcome, with paid street parking available nearby, making it convenient to visit us for your massage treatment.
