About Nutcha Thai Massage

At Nutcha Thai Massage, we're passionate about bringing authentic Thai healing traditions to communities across the UK. Frequently Asked Questions

  • For traditional Thai massage, wear loose, comfortable clothing. For oil-based treatments like hot oil or deep tissue massage, you'll undress to your comfort level and be properly draped with towels throughout the session.

  • For general wellness and relaxation, once a month is ideal. If addressing specific issues like chronic pain or stress, weekly sessions may be beneficial initially. For maintenance, every 2-4 weeks works well for most clients.

  • Your therapist will discuss any health concerns, problem areas, and treatment preferences. Sessions typically last 60-90 minutes. You may feel slightly tender afterwards, which is normal. Drink plenty of water and rest when possible.

  • You may feel deeply relaxed, slightly tender, or energised. Some people experience mild soreness the next day as toxins are released. Drink plenty of water, avoid alcohol, and allow time to rest and integrate the treatment benefits.

  • While we accept walk-ins subject to availability, we recommend booking online or by phone to secure your preferred appointment time. This ensures you get the treatment and therapist you want at a convenient time.

  • All Nutcha Thai Massage locations are open Monday to Sunday, 10am to 8pm. We welcome walk-ins, but online booking is preferred to guarantee your preferred time slot and therapist availability.
